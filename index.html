Welcome to Aura Lough Nails

Located in the heart of Loughborough, Aura Beauty Lounge combines modern techniques, quality products, and a peaceful atmosphere to create a truly enjoyable self-care experience. We are committed to providing excellent customer service, maintaining high standards of hygiene, and ensuring every client feels comfortable and cared for from the moment they walk through our doors.

At Aura Beauty Lounge, we believe self-care should be both relaxing and luxurious. Our lounge was created to provide a calm and welcoming space where clients can enjoy high-quality nail services and soothing Head Spa experiences designed to help them look and feel their best.

We specialise in elegant manicures, spa pedicures, nail artistry, and relaxing Head Spa treatments that promote scalp health, healthy hair, and overall wellbeing. From deep scalp cleansing and nourishing treatments to therapeutic scalp massages, every service is carefully designed to leave you feeling refreshed, confident, and fully recharged.

Whether you’re visiting for beautiful nails or a calming Head Spa escape, Aura Beauty Lounge is your destination for relaxation, care, and confidence.
